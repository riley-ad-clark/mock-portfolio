About Me :

Hi again! As you know, my name is Riley Clark, you also know that I am an aspiring full-stack developer. But you're here to learn more about me, not what you already know!

I was born, raised, and currently reside in Winnipeg, Manitoba. At first, I couldn't have fathomed entering the programming space to the extent in which I have. As a matter of fact, I initially wanted to be a graphic designer, but as things have a tendency to do... that didn't necessarily work out. But fear not, it gave way to this glorious site of mine! So, in the end, we all won in one way or another.

I am an avid enjoyer of video games, music, and movies, virtually anything to wind down at the end of the day. And, last but not least, I adore all aspects (both good, and bad) of coding. As with many other things you will eventually learn within your lifetime, it allows you to be a life long learner, which is something we can all attest is a good thing in the end!
